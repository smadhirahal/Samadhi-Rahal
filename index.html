About Me

I’m Samadhi Rahal Samarasinghe — an editor, artist, and content-maker who believes good work starts with respect for the craft. I draw and paint, edit cinematic videos, and help creators present their best work online. I learned to value the old-school methods because they teach discipline; I use modern tools because they let the vision breathe.

work covers gaming and tech videos, cinematic edits, and polished photo and motion projects. I push for clarity and depth: no flashy shortcuts, just choices that actually serve the story. I question every trend — it keeps me honest, and it keeps the output useful tomorrow, not just loud today.

If you want edits that look good and mean something, or art that’s been built, not slapped together, send a note. I collaborate, edit, and make things that outlast trends. — Samadhi
